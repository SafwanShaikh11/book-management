The

Legendary E46 M3 GTR

The E46 M3 GTR is a legendary race car developed by BMW for the American Le Mans Series (ALMS) in 2001. It stands out with a powerful 4.0-liter V8 engine (P60B40), a significant departure from the standard E46 M3's inline-six engine. The GTR featured extensive weight reduction, widebody aerodynamics, and enhanced track performance. A few street-legal versions were produced to meet racing regulations, making them extremely rare. The E46 M3 GTR is celebrated for its dominance on the track and remains one of the most iconic BMW race cars in history.
