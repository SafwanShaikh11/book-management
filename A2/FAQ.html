How Long Does it Take to Charge an Electric BMW??

Most all-electric BMW models recharge their batteries at rates between 30 to a maximum of 108 miles per hour, depending on whether you're using a home, Level 2, or DC fast charger. The home and Level 2 chargers are often the most convenient options, and DC fast charging is an excellent way to recharge your battery quickly while on the go. You can also recharge up to 4 miles an hour when using any common 120-volt outlet..
